This workflow is an intelligent lead management agent that automatically captures, analyzes, and routes incoming leads based on their intent. 

When a user submits the form, the system uses AI to evaluate the message and classify the lead as Hot or Cold: 

Hot Leads (high intent / business inquiries) 
→ Instantly saved in Zoho CRM 
→ Automatic confirmation email sent to the lead 

Cold Leads (low intent / general inquiries) 
→ Stored in Google Sheets for nurturing and follow-up 

The system also ensures no duplicate entries by validating leads based on email and phone number, keeping your database clean and efficient. 

Overall, this automation helps businesses: 

Prioritize high-value leads 

Respond instantly 

Maintain organized and deduplicated lead data 

Reduce manual work with AI-driven decision-making
